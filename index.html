Wake up + get ready 🌤️

Not aggressively early, but not sleeping the day away either.

10:30 am–12:00 pm

We can Uber to Sabine. It’s the cute Saturday brunch choice: pretty, polished, and very girls-trip coded.

After Sabine, we can do a little pier-area moment, walk for a bit, or take a Lime bike/scooter for fun before heading toward the next stop.

1:00–1:45 pm

1:45–2:00 pm

A cute little transition if everyone is down. Fun, easy, and keeps the vibe going.

2:00–3:30 pm

3:30–5:15 pm
